quiz rules - how it works:

Test your knowledge with this interactive quiz! Each question will be presented one at a time, and you can select your answer before proceeding to the next. Please note that once you lock in an answer and move to the next question, you won't be able to return to previous questions, so choose wisely. At the end of the quiz, your results will be displayed, including your score, the number of correct and incorrect answers, and performance feedback. Good luck, and enjoy!
